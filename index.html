🧮 The Mathematics

We model the charging RC circuit using the standard first-order ODE:

$$ R \frac{dq}{dt} + \frac{q}{C} = V(t) $$

Given $q = C \cdot V_c$, substituting gives:

$$ RC \frac{dV_c}{dt} + V_c = V_s $$

The analytical solution evaluates to:

$$ V_c(t) = V_s \left(1 - e^{-\frac{t}{RC}}\right) $$
